CERTIFIER - REQUIREMENTS

General information ‚úÖ

A tool that allows students to generate their certificate of completion of a given online course üèÜ

An admin defines the template and a list of people who finished the course üìù

Students open a given link and their certificate is generated üîó

As an admin

  • I want to choose a template for a certificate
  • I want to fill in the course information
  • I want to upload the list of students eligible for certification

Certificate Templates

  • Should contain the course name
  • Should contain the logo of the institution (course issuer)
  • Should contain the length of the course in hours

As a student

  • I receive the email with the link
  • I open the link
  • Ta-da my certificate is generated
  • Now I can print it or download as a PDF

The requirements are not written in stone
